Key Insights
💡
TikTok dominates reach at 70.8% of impressions
With 28.7M impressions vs Google's 7.2M and Facebook's 4.5M, TikTok delivers the widest top-of-funnel coverage — ideal for awareness at scale. Its CPM of $2.59 is the lowest across all platforms.
🎯
Google Search Brand Terms wins on conversion efficiency
At $5.10 CPA, Search Brand Terms is the best-performing campaign overall. Facebook Retargeting follows at $5.95 CPA — both significantly undercut TikTok's Traffic Campaign at $14.06 CPA.
⚠️
Google Generic Search has 4× higher CPA than brand terms
At $24.80 CPA, Search_Generic_Terms is a budget drain. With 627 conversions on $15.5K spend, reallocating even 30% to Brand Terms or Shopping could yield ~200 additional conversions.
